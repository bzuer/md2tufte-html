## About

Journalist and social scientist. Doctoral researcher in the Graduate Program in Social Anthropology at the National Museum, Federal University of Rio de Janeiro.

The doctoral research focuses on understanding Western Thought in its Modern type. To achieve this, it pursues Max Weber's historical-terminological constructions.

Responsible for the Ethnos project, which seeks to gather a large portion of bibliographic production in Social Anthropology for bibliographic consultation and thematic research of interest, in an open and simplified manner.

Ongoing works include the Portuguese translation of Max Weber’s "Gesammelte Aufsätze zur Religionssoziologie" (Collected Essays in the Sociology of Religion).
